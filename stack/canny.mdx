Canny.io is a product feedback management platform that enables businesses to collect and manage customer feedback. It provides a simple and intuitive interface that allows users to submit feedback, vote on existing feedback, and track its progress. Canny.io helps businesses prioritize their product development efforts based on the most important feedback from their customers.

With Canny.io, businesses can create a public roadmap that displays their plans for product development, giving customers visibility into what features are coming next. Businesses can also use Canny.io to communicate with customers directly through comments and notifications, providing a seamless feedback loop.
